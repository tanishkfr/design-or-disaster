Arc's model for browsers is finally making sense

By Nilay Patel  ·  Jun 9, 2024  ·  6 min read
Arc Browser, The Browser Company — macOS, 2024

The Browser Company has been asking an uncomfortable question for three years: what if the web browser you use every day was designed around finishing things rather than accumulating them?

Arc organises your browsing into Spaces, with a sidebar that separates permanent fixtures from today's session. When you close a tab and come back the next day, some things are still there. Others are not. The model has a logic to it that takes about a week to trust.
